QCY Melobuds HT05 Documentation
Overview Features Battery Life Connectivity Controls
Overview

The QCY Melobuds HT05 are true wireless earbuds designed for immersive audio experiences and reliable connectivity. With active noise cancellation, touch controls, and customizable settings, these earbuds are suited for both casual and intense listening sessions.

They come with customizable sound profiles, supporting a range of frequencies for better listening experiences. The model also has an advanced low-latency mode for gaming.

Features

Key features include active noise cancellation, water resistance, and touch-sensitive controls for play, pause, and voice assistant activation.

The earbuds offer dual connectivity options for seamless device switching.

  • Active Noise Cancellation
  • Water and sweat resistance
  • Touch controls for easy operation
  • Customizable sound profiles
Battery Life

The QCY Melobuds HT05 offers up to 6 hours of playtime on a single charge, with an additional 24 hours provided by the charging case.

The earbuds support fast charging, delivering 1 hour of playtime with just a 10-minute charge.

Connectivity

These earbuds support Bluetooth 5.2 for reliable, low-latency connections and enhanced sound quality.

The connection range extends up to 10 meters, allowing freedom of movement.

Controls

The earbuds feature intuitive touch controls for music, calls, and voice assistant activation. Each control is managed by a tap or hold command on the earbuds.

By tapping or holding the earbuds, users can control playback, adjust volume, or switch modes.

  • Tap to play/pause
  • Double-tap for next track
  • Hold to activate noise cancellation
